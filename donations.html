What Fuse Techni NPC does:

Fuse Techni is a technology-driven organisation that specializes in uplifting young people through digital skills. It does this by targeting young adults living in underprivileged communities who are unemployed and seeking employment opportunities in the tech economy.

Why donations matter?

Since Fuse Techni NPC is a non-profit company/organisation, shareholders do not profit off the organisation instead, they are dependent on the little funds they get from the government and from their own pockets. Your donations will help Fuse Techni go a long way, by helping us upgrade our facilities, expanding throughout, help our youth reach their goals through our platform and raeching their desired goals.
